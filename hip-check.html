How It Works

1. Set phone on tripod ~8 ft behind you, down the line, hip height.

2. Hit Start Session — a countdown gives you time to walk out and get set.

3. App auto-detects your hips and locks baseline. You'll hear a chime.

4. Swing away — instant audio feedback on every swing. Fully hands-free.

Voice commands: say "recalibrate" to re-lock baseline, or "stop" to end.
