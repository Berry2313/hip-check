How It Works

1. Set phone on tripod ~8 ft behind you, down the target line, hip height.

2. Hit Start Session — the pose model loads, then a countdown gives you time to get set.

3. App uses AI pose detection to find your hip keypoints and lock your baseline at address.

4. Swing away — tracks your actual hip position every frame. Instant audio feedback.

Voice commands: say "recalibrate" to re-lock, "stop" to end session.
